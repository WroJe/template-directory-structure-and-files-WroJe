Electro Theremin Project

By Sanjay Raj and Jeffrey Robinson-Wrobel

- The Electro Theremin -

This clock displays the current time in 24 hours. Ex. 13:25 is the same as 1:25 PM. To convert 24 hour time into 12 hour time deduct 12 hours and visa versa.

The materials consist of:

  • An arduino board (any model will suffice)
  • Some wires for cabling
  • A box made out of your choice of material. We chose pexiglass as our box model
  • an assortment of LED lights (again any kind will work)
  • 200 Ohm resitors
  • A small micro usb cable to plug in to the computer
  • Some basic knowlage of C or C++ derivative
